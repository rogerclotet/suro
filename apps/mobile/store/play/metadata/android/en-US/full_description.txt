Suro is a shared corkboard for the people you do life with. Lists, calendar, notes, files and shared expenses for your group, in one calm place — so you can stop juggling chats, spreadsheets and reminders.

LISTS
Shopping lists, packing lists, to-dos. Organize items into categories, save reusable templates, and check things off together in real time.

CALENDAR
Plan trips, dinners and birthdays. Link a list or a note to an event so the packing or the shopping is right where you need it. Subscribe from Google Calendar or Apple Calendar.

NOTES & FILES
Share rich-text notes, photos and PDFs with the whole group. No more digging through chat history.

SHARED EXPENSES
Track who paid for what, split the bill fairly, and let Suro work out the simplest way to settle up.

STAY IN THE LOOP
Get notified when someone updates a list, adds an event, or settles a debt. No chat-app noise.

MADE FOR THE GROUPS IN YOUR LIFE
• Flatmates — keep the shopping, chores and bills under control, together, not just in one person's head.
• Families — from weekly meal planning to holiday packing, share what matters with the people at home.
• Friend groups — plan the trip and split the costs without endless group-chat scrolling.

Start your first group in under a minute: sign in with Google or your email, create a group, and invite your people with a link. No credit card, no setup.

Suro doesn't sell your data and runs no algorithmic feeds.
